What is a Mastermind meeting?

A Mastermind meeting is a GREAT opportunity to sit and discuss your programming journey and projects with people who are just as passionate as you are about the topic!

Register Now!!

Meeting's AGENDA!

The meeting consists of three parts:

  1. Each attendee describes what they're working on.
  2. One of the attendees leads the "Hot Seat" in which they can discuss an interesting idea or project with the rest of the attendees.
  3. Each attendee decides on what their objective is for the coming week.

Ground rules:

In all of our meetings,
we follow these rules:

  • No interruptions.
  • We show respect for each other.
  • Phones are on silent mode.
  • We stick to the time.
  • Each meeting has its leader.
  • We must attend all the meetings.
